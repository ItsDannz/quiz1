Local Food

Gudeg

Gudeg is an invitation to enjoy the true flavors of Indonesia, bringing nostalgia and delight in every portion. Ideal for a special lunch or dinner.

Kemiri Soto

Kemiri Soto, This is not an ordinary soto, this is a legendary dish that combines the uniqueness of candlenut spices with a wealth of herbs, creating a flavor harmony that you will not forget.

Gembong Tape

Gembong Tape, a piece of story and warmth from Pati. Perfect as a special souvenir for family and friends, or as a companion for your tea/coffee every day.
