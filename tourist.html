Tourist Places

Omah Kayu

Omah Kayu, where traditional ambiance meets unforgettable culinary delights. Our open-air "Wooden House" restaurant offers a truly authentic dining experience, perfect for families, friends, and anyone craving a taste of Indonesia's rich culinary heritage.

Waduk Gembong

Waduk Gembong, where nature's beauty unfolds. Escape the ordinary and immerse yourself in the breathtaking tranquility of Waduk Gembong. This stunning reservoir is a hidden gem, offering a panoramic canvas of nature's finest artistry, especially as the sun dips below the horizon.

Jimbaran Cave

Jimbaran Cave, a journey into earth's masterpiece. Step into a world of enchanting mystery and natural splendor at Gua Jimbaran. This captivating cave system invites you to embark on an unforgettable adventure, revealing the Earth's ancient secrets and breathtaking beauty.
