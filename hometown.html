Hometown

Pati, My Hometown

Pati is a regency in Central Java, well known for its slogan “Pati Bumi Mina Tani”, which reflects the community’s strengths in agriculture and fisheries. Also nicknamed the City of Peanuts for its renowned peanut industry, Pati is rich in history, culture, and local wisdom, making it a place with a unique identity and strong character.
